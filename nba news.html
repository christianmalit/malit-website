LeBron James Medical Update Official Lakers Release
Posted: Jan 16, 2019
FACEBOOK GOOGLE PLUS TWITTER
El Segundo - LeBron James was evaluated by team medical staff and physicians today. James, who will not travel with the team on the upcoming two-game road trip, has been cleared to return to practice commencing next week, and progress towards a return to game play thereafter.

                                                                 The Philippine Basketball Association (PBA) News


    

MANILA, Philippines -- Robert Bolick had a superb debut to power the NorthPort Batang Pier to a comfortable 117-91 victory over the Blackwater Elite in their first game of the 2019 PBA Philippine Cup on Wednesday at the Araneta Coliseum. In his first game as a professional, Bolick -- the third overall pick in the 2018 PBA Rookie Draft -- scored 26 points on 10-of-12 shooting, going a perfect 7-of-7 from inside the arc. Five other players scored in double-digits for the Batang Pier, with Paolo Taha notching 21 points, and Stanley Pringle adding 16 markers on top of 7 rebounds, 6 steals, and 5 assists. Sean Anthony added 19 points. "It's a total team effort," said NorthPort coach Pido Jarencio after the game. "Lahat, they did their share." "We're making our shots, 'yung decision-making, maganda," he added. "We're complementing each other, nagpapasahan, we're getting rebounds. We're running. 'Yan ang basketball namin."
